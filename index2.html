An actuary's job responsibilities.

Requirements

To become an actuary, a person needs a bachelor's degree in actuarial science, mathematics, statistics, or a related field. Actuaries need to have analytical problem solving skills, math and numeracy skills, computer skills, knowledge of business and finance, and cost-benefit analyst skills.

The University of Chicago is the number one school for Actuarial Science in Illinois and third best school in the United States.
